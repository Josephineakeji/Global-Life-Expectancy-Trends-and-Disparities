## Project Summary

Analyzed global life expectancy data using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover **trends and inequalities** across regions, income levels, and gender.

Designed an interactive dashboard that transforms raw data into **clear, actionable insights for decision-making**.

---

## Business Problem

Life expectancy is a critical indicator of a nation’s **health, development, and quality of life**.

This project answers:

* How has global life expectancy evolved over time?
* Where do disparities exist across regions?
* Impact of income levels on life expectancy and gender gap

---

## Dataset

**Life Expectancy at Birth (2000-2021)**

* Covers global, regional, and economic group classifications
* Includes gender-based estimates
* Represents the **average lifespan of a newborn**

## Dashboards & Key Insights 

### 1. Global Trend (2000–2021)

<img width="499" height="173" alt="global_change" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b5b557a-7f98-406d-b46f-3233117f90e7" /> <img width="166" height="133" alt="gender" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff3ece42-fc5c-4754-840f-8a6f19f6762b" />

**Insights**
* Life expectancy increased steadily for nearly two decades
* Reflects improvements in **healthcare and living standards globally**
* A noticeable decline occurred in 2020 and 2021, likely driven by:
  **increased global mortality during the pandemic**
* A comparism between 2000 and 2021 shows a significant increase in life expectancy at birth
* Females outlive males across all years

---

## Limitations

* Dataset does not include:

  * Mortality rates
  * Healthcare access
  * Environmental factors
* Some insights are **inferred due to missing variables**

---

## Key Insights for Decision-Making

* Life expectancy strongly correlates with **quality of life and economic status**
* Addressing inequality requires:

  * Investment in healthcare in developing regions
  * Improved infrastructure in developing regions
  * Better living conditions in low-income regions
